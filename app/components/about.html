About the EBS Symposium

The EBS Symposium is one of Europe’s largest and most prestigious student-run business conferences. With a history spanning 27 years, the EBS Symposium offers speeches, workshops, a recruiting and an innovation fair. This three-day event gathers about 1000 participants from the best universities and companies in Europe, addressing topics relevant to business and politics.
